# About BUBU

## Company Identity

**BUBU.com** is a **Cultural Intelligence Agency**, operating under the holding name **Cultural Continuum Co.**

- **Founded**: 1996 (30+ years of operation)
- **HQ**: Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Expanding to**: Dubai
- **Contact**: hello@bubu.com / +6221 38808185

BUBU positions itself not as a traditional agency, but as a cultural intelligence network — tapping into subcultures, youth culture, and innovation ecosystems to uncover what moves people today and what will matter tomorrow.

> *"It's not about visibility. It's about relevance."*

---

## Core Philosophy

**Cultural Intelligence** is BUBU's core differentiator — the ability to understand what people feel, share, and believe *before* it evolves into a social movement.

**The BUBU Edge:**
- **Culture** — deep immersion in communities and subcultures
- **Data** — tracking signals, patterns, behavioral shifts
- **Creativity** — translating insight into compelling experiences
- **Technology** — AI and Web3 to amplify intelligence and reach

**Working Method:** Deep Immerse → Strategic Curation → Measured Impact

**Brand-to-Culture Framework:**
1. **Belief** — Define what the brand truly stands for
2. **Signal** — Identify what communities care about and why
3. **Moment** — Design an immersive, shareable brand experience
4. **Movement** — Activate audiences through participation
5. **Legacy** — Shape conversations and long-term cultural value

## Working Context for Claude

When assisting with BUBU work, keep in mind:

- BUBU is not a traditional ad agency — frame everything through **cultural relevance**, not just reach or impressions
- The company operates across **multiple sub-brands** — understand which unit a task belongs to (LabX for collaborations, BUBU Gaming for esports, Kisah Visual for AI content, etc.)
- The audience is **Indonesia-first but globally ambitious** — communications often need to balance local cultural nuance with international appeal
- BUBU emphasizes **community, movement, and legacy** over one-time campaigns
- AI and storytelling are core tools — **Kisah Visual** is the AI-forward content arm
- The team works across digital marketing, content production, web/app development, PR, branding, and event/experiential activation
