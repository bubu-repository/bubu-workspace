# LabX Con 2026: Pre-Event Activation Program

**Source:** LabX Con Master Deck 2026 (Refine), slides 35, 48, 57, 76, 80, 82-83
**Tagline:** CONNECT. COLLABORATE. BUILD THE MOMENTUM.
**Window:** June-October 2026, leading into the November main event
**Date:** 2026-07-07

Three activation formats warm up the ecosystem before doors open. Each has a defined purpose, flow, and partner package. The strategic job is the same across all three: turn the collaboration process itself into visible, engaging content that builds anticipation toward the final output.

---

## 1. Padel Networking & Collaboration Workshop

**Purpose (three-part, from the deck):**
1. Build awareness around collaboration culture: why brands now grow through shared audiences, communities, and cultural relevance rather than traditional marketing alone.
2. Create brand-to-brand connections in a relaxed, informal environment.
3. Build ecosystem through shared experience: sport as the social bridge before deeper conversations about creativity, collaboration, and the future LabX ecosystem.

**Event flow:**
1. **Padel Networking Session:** curated fun matches between selected brands and collaborators; natural interaction, casual relationship-building.
2. **Collaboration Workshop:** intimate sharing session on why collaboration matters now, collaboration trends and case studies, and how brands grow through ecosystem and culture.
3. **Networking & Ecosystem Gathering:** open-ended session to continue conversations and build connections inside the LabX ecosystem.

**Partner package (per deck):** featured as part of the official pre-event ecosystem; brand visibility across selected pre-event communication materials; inclusion in workshop and networking documentation; photo and video documentation; social media recap; collaborative content opportunities during the sessions.

## 2. In-House Session (Private Brand Session)

**Format:** an exclusive in-house collaboration session designed for one brand at a time. Focused, intimate, premium.

**Mechanics:** LabX Con curates and hosts a dedicated session directly at the brand's office, space, or chosen venue, tailored to the brand's vision, challenges, and collaboration opportunities.

**Workshop content:** the importance of collaboration in today's landscape; how brands build relevance through culture and community; collaboration trends, insights, and case studies; potential collaboration opportunities within the LabX ecosystem.

**Strategic access:** direct introduction to selected collaborators, creators, and ecosystem players; early exploration of partnership and activation opportunities before public phases open.

## 3. LabX Space, Blok M (3-Day Curated Compound)

**Format:** a curated pre-event hub running 3 days at Blok M, featuring collaboration teasers, installations, brand showcases, and community-driven activations leading up to LabX Con.

**Title-sponsor pre-event rights (Founding Partner tier):**
- Full control of LabX Space with a signature installation
- High-frequency exposure before the main event; positioned as the main driver of pre-event attention
- Teaser, build-up, and behind-the-scenes content; story development for the brand x artist collaboration
- Early collaboration phase: initiate the brand x artist collaboration before the event and turn the process into a visible journey
- Activation through selected communities and KOLs; organic amplification through creators and collaborators

Session concepts already surfaced: "Networking & Fashion Soiree," "The Heart of Muslim Branding," "How AI Tools are Transforming Fashion and Branding" (with @hugastyle), "The ReWorkshop." Reuse these as programming seeds for the June-October calendar.
