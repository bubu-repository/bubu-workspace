# LabX Con 2026: Commercial Model, Sponsorship Tiers, and P&L

**Source:** LabX Con Master Deck 2026 (Refine), slides 31-34, 73-95, 106-107
**Status:** Internal commercial reference. Confidential; numbers are deck projections, not commitments.
**Date:** 2026-07-07

The business case is direct: total cost projection Rp 9.0B against revenue scenarios of Rp 15.77B to Rp 17.77B, netting Rp 6.77B to Rp 8.77B. Sponsorship is the core revenue engine (Rp 13.5B of every scenario); ticketing and merchandising are secondary layers.

---

## 1. Partnership Tiers (Partner-Facing)

| Tier | Investment | Identity line | Headline benefits |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Founding Partner** (Title Sponsor) | IDR 5,000,000,000 | Lead the ecosystem. Own the stage. | Full naming rights ("LabX Con Presented by Brand"); main entrance takeover and hero installation; end-to-end co-creation with top-tier artists; dedicated installation alongside the 5 Design Houses; full KOL ecosystem (nano to macro); O2O integration, interaction tracking, post-event insights; pre-event Title activation (LabX Space takeover, teaser and behind-the-scenes narrative) |
| **Strategic Partner** (Co-Sponsor) | IDR 1,000,000,000 / brand | Play your part. Grow together. | Co-created activation with selected artists; dedicated branded installation or zone; medium-format visibility; featured across LabX channels, website, and commerce platform; curated KOL network (nano to micro); QR-based engagement tracking and basic performance reporting |
| **Supporting Sponsor** | IDR 500,000,000 / brand | Targeted integration. | Inclusion within selected installations and collaboration outputs; standard branding across touchpoints, grouped placement in high-traffic areas; inclusion in LabX content ecosystem and KOL seeding; QR interaction and basic commerce access |
| **Collaboration Partner** | IDR 100,000,000 | Create together. Be part of the culture. | Curated brand x artist match with concept development support; one dedicated collaboration space (installation or showcase); integration within one of the 5 Design House islands; campaign and on-site branding; direct Gen Z / culture-driven audience access; automatic BUBU Awards eligibility |

Note the deck's two framings: the 3-tier partner-facing story (5B / 1B / 100M, "three levels of partnership") and the 4-tier revenue model that adds Supporting Sponsors at 500M. Use the 3-tier story in narrative slides, the 4-tier grid in commercial negotiations.

## 2. Revenue Scenarios

**A. Sponsorship (core revenue, identical in all scenarios): Rp 13.5B**
- Title Sponsor: 1 x Rp 7.0B = Rp 7.0B
- Co-Sponsor: 4 x Rp 1.0B = Rp 4.0B
- Supporting Sponsor: 5 x Rp 500M = Rp 2.5B

(Deck note: Title slot priced at Rp 7B in the revenue model versus Rp 5B in the tier description; treat 5B as floor, 7B as target.)

**B. Ticketing (all scenarios): Rp 1.09B**
- Day 1: 3,000 pax x Rp 70,000 = Rp 210M
- Days 2-3: 6,000 pax x Rp 80,000 = Rp 480M
- 3-Day Pass bundle: 2,000 pax x Rp 200,000 = Rp 400M

**C. Merchandising & tenant layer (the variable):**

| Scenario | Mechanic | Merch layer | Total revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Option A** | Booth & activation fee: 25 installations x Rp 15-20M + F&B stalls (Rp 6M/tenant) + Rp 500M apparel merch | ± Rp 1.06-1.18B | **Rp 15.77B** |
| **Option B** | Revenue share 15% x sales (assumes each installation sells Rp 500M of goods) + F&B + merch | Rp 2.56B | **Rp 17.15B** |
| **Option C** | Brand collaboration fee: 25 x Rp 100M + F&B + merch | Rp 3.18B | **Rp 17.77B** |

## 3. Cost Structure (Projection: Rp 9.0B envelope)

**OPEX (higher scenario Rp 4.0B; lean scenario Rp 3.2B):** project management Rp 800M; event crew and ops Rp 300-600M; licensing and permits Rp 200M; security and safety (non-venue) Rp 300M; conference/program ops Rp 200-400M; ticketing and system Rp 200M; transport and accommodation Rp 100-300M; F&B internal Rp 200M; documentation and media Rp 300M; misc buffer Rp 500-700M.

**Venue & production (higher scenario Rp 3.5B; lean scenario Rp 1.95B):** PIM 3 Sky Level rental Rp 300M-1.5B; electricity and utilities Rp 300M; cleaning Rp 50-100M; venue security Rp 50-150M; booth construction Rp 550M; flooring and layout Rp 100-150M; main stage Rp 200M; sound Rp 150M; lighting Rp 100M; LED screen Rp 50-150M; rigging and truss Rp 50-100M; internet Rp 50M.

## 4. P&L Scenarios (Deck Projection)

| Metric | Option A | Option B | Option C |
|---|---|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Rp 15.77B | Rp 17.15B | Rp 17.77B |
| Total Cost | Rp 9.0B | Rp 9.0B | Rp 9.0B |

(The deck's margin percentages do not follow from profit/revenue, which would be 42.9% / 47.5% / 49.3%; they appear to be profit/cost. Flag before any external use.)

## 5. Sales Priorities

1. Close the Title slot first; it anchors Rp 7B and the naming story.
2. Fill 4 Co-Sponsor slots across non-competing categories (deck brand pool spans automotive/EV, banking, logistics, sportswear, F&B, gaming, watches, fragrance).
3. Choose the merch mechanic (A fee-based, B revenue-share, C collab-fee) before tenant contracting; Option C yields most but requires 25 committed collaboration fees.
4. Supporting Sponsor and Collaboration Partner tiers backfill; Collaboration Partner (100M) is the volume product with 25 slots implied.
