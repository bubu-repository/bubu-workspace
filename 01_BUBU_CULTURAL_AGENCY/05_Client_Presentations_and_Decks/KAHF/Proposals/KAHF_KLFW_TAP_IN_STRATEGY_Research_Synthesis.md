## VALIDATED TAP-IN IDEAS (From Fashion Week Research)

### Tap-In #1: BACKSTAGE GROOMING FACILITATION
**What works at fashion weeks:**
- K18 Hair Flip Challenge (NYFW 2026): 1.9M impressions, 5,000 samples
- Dieux × Eckhaus Latta (NYFW SS26): Eye patches worn ON RUNWAY as part of show aesthetic
- TRESemmé A-List Salon (NYFW 2026): Generated $6.2M EMV + 140% month-over-month engagement surge
- **Key principle:** Beauty prep becomes part of the SHOW, not hidden backstage

**How KAHF taps in at Todak's segment:**

**The Setup:**
- KAHF sponsors the "Grooming Prep Station" backstage during Todak's Friday 4 PM runway
- Visible but not obtrusive: KAHF grooming specialist + 1 assistant at backstage entrance
- All 24 Todak models receive KAHF face prep (5-min touch-up: cleanser + moisturizer + SPF)
- Signage: Clean, minimal—"Models prepared with KAHF Grooming System"

**Why it works:**
- Models' skin is visibly fresh + glowing (product proof)
- 24 models × multiple photos = guaranteed brand visibility in runway photography
- Todak's backstage team sees KAHF in action (professional, seamless)
- Photographers/media capture behind-the-scenes grooming moments

**Budget:** 35–50M IDR
- 1 KAHF grooming specialist (salary + accommodation + airfare): 15–20M IDR
- Product supply (generous amounts for 24 models): 10–15M IDR
- Signage + setup: 5–10M IDR

**Content goldmine:**
- 5–10 professional behind-the-scenes photos (models being groomed → styled → runway)
- UGC from backstage crew/photographers: "Models groomed with KAHF"
- Post-event: "Meet the grooming team behind Todak's runway" social series

**Risk:** Low. Enhances Todak's show; doesn't interfere with production.

### Tap-In #2: VIP GROOMING LOUNGE (Post-Runway Engagement)
**What works at fashion weeks:**
- Dior Beauty Millefiori Garden (NYFW 2021): Sold out, generated massive organic TikTok engagement; "designed for digital content"
- TRESemmé A-List Salon: 6-day activation, appointment-based access, then public (Valentine's Day strategy)
- Rare Beauty Pop-Up (NYC 2023): Color-coded environment (pink), Instagram-worthy mirrors, high-touch experience

**How KAHF taps in at Todak's guests:**

**The Setup:**
- KAHF curates "Cultured Grooming Lounge" in Showspace (200–300 sq ft) during/after Todak runway
- Location: Adjacent to Todak's VIP seating or showspace exit (capture guests post-show)
- Open: 1–2 hours post-runway (5–7 PM Friday)
- Capacity: Designed to flow 200–400 guests (Todak's 350 + KLFW walk-by traffic)

**Experience Design:**
1. **Grooming Consultation Station** (2 stations, 5–10 min per guest)
   - KAHF grooming consultant (professional, trained)
   - Skin type assessment (30 seconds: oily, combination, dry)
   - Product recommendation + demo (cleanser or serum matched to need)
   - Complimentary sample (travel-size face wash or serum)

2. **Touch-Up Bar** (Optional)
   - Beard grooming touch-up (beard balm, grooming oil)
   - Skin refresh: hydrating mist + SPF reapplication
   - 3–5 min service; high-touch, memorable

3. **Photo Moment** (Instagram-worthy)
   - Styled mirror setup with soft lighting
   - Simple branding: "KAHF Cultured Grooming" text + Todak collaboration visual
   - Guests take selfies with product (natural UGC)
   - KAHF staff optional: gently guide guests for best angle (not intrusive)

4. **Sampling & Gifting Hub**
   - "Cultured Grooming Kit" (3-piece): face wash, moisturizer, fragrance sample
   - Presentation: Branded cardboard sleeve (minimal but premium feel)
   - 200–300 kits prepared (covers expected lounge traffic)

**Why it works:**
- **Low friction:** No appointment needed; drop-in experience fits post-runway energy
- **Memorable:** Personal grooming consultation = premium positioning (not just retail)
- **Content:** Guests naturally take photos at mirror setup; UGC with KAHF branding visible
- **Influencer honey pot:** Malaysian beauty/lifestyle influencers attending KLFW will document the lounge
- **Retail conversation starter:** Guests learn about KAHF in context of "premium lifestyle" (not e-commerce)

**Budget:** 80–120M IDR
- 2 grooming consultants (salary + accommodation): 30–40M IDR
- Setup/design (lounge fabrication, mirrors, lighting): 25–35M IDR
- Product inventory (sampling + kits): 20–30M IDR
- Signage + minor ambiance: 5–10M IDR

**Content goldmine:**
- 30–50 UGC posts from guests (selfies at mirror with KAHF branding)
- 5–10 influencer posts from Malaysian accounts attending KLFW
- Behind-the-scenes video: consultants in action
- Post-event: "200+ guests experienced KAHF's grooming philosophy at KLFW" testimonial content

**Risk:** Medium. Requires trained staff (quality control critical) + lounge setup complexity. But proven model at NYFW.

### Tap-In #3: CO-BRANDED "CULTURED GROOMING" MOMENT (Premium, Signature)
**What works at fashion weeks:**
- Merit × Brandon Maxwell collaboration (2023+): 30,000+ waitlist signups in 5 days; 30% higher conversion rate

- K18 Guinness World Record activation: Viral social moment + $100K charity tie-in = earned media goldmine

**How KAHF taps in at Todak's afterparty:**

**The Setup:**
- Planned co-branded moment during Todak's afterparty (post-runway DJ celebration)
- Timeline: ~7 PM Friday (peak energy, models + key guests present)
- Central moment: Models exit staging area → "KAHF Grooming Moment" → Reappear styled & groomed for final photos

**The Execution:**
1. **Visual Storytelling (Staged)**
   - Todak models in showcase outfits enter "KAHF Grooming Pod" (small branded setup)
   - KAHF grooming team does live, visible grooming prep (2–3 min per model)
   - Models reappear polished, fresh-faced, glowing
   - Photo op: Models + Todak designer + KAHF grooming specialist together

2. **Limited-Edition Co-Branded Gift**
   - "KAHF × Todak Cultured Grooming Kit" (500 units)
   - Contents: Face cleanser (KAHF signature), moisturizer serum, fragrance sample
   - Packaging: Minimal, premium cardboard sleeve with both logos (not tacky; subtle co-branding)
   - Distribution: VIPs + influencers + event attendees

3. **Social Media Amplification (Real-Time)**
   - KLFW official account posts: "Witness the grooming moment: KAHF x Todak collaboration live at the afterparty"
   - Todak posts behind-the-scenes grooming → styled transition
   - KAHF posts: "Grooming is the final step of style" + footage of models being prepped
   - Hashtag: #KAHFxTodakCultured (seed with 5–10 Malaysian influencers attending)

4. **Charity/Narrative Tie-In** (Optional but recommended)
   - "For every KAHF × Todak kit distributed, KAHF donates [X amount] to [local youth grooming education initiative]"
   - Generates earned media beyond just brand mention

**Why it works:**
- **Newsworthy:** Co-branded moment = press-worthy (not standard sponsorship)
- **Narrative:** "Grooming is part of the look" = positions KAHF as lifestyle authority, not cosmetics brand
- **Influencer goldmine:** Malaysian beauty/fashion influencers will cover a co-branded designer moment
- **Post-event asset:** Limited-edition kit becomes collectible; social proof for Malaysia market entry
- **Drives retail conversation:** Watsons/Guardian Malaysia can reference "Limited KLFW Edition" when KAHF approaches for shelf space

**Budget:** 120–180M IDR
- Limited-edition kit production (500 units, design + packaging + assembly): 40–60M IDR
- Professional photography/videography (2–3 hours coverage): 20–30M IDR
- Influencer seeding fees (5 tier-1 Malaysian accounts @ 15–25M each): 75–125M IDR
- Charity tie-in (if pursued): 10–20M IDR
- Creative direction + social amplification: 5–10M IDR

**Content goldmine:**
- 1–2 professional moment photos (models + designer + grooming specialist, styled, high-production)
- 5–10 behind-the-scenes grooming transition clips (Reels/TikTok format)
- 5–8 influencer features (Malaysian beauty/fashion accounts covering the moment)
- Press coverage: "Indonesian premium skincare brand debuts in Malaysia at KLFW"
- Post-event: Kit unboxing videos, testimonial content ("I got the limited KLFW edition")

**Risk:** Medium-High. Requires clear creative direction + influencer management. But highest impact for brand lift + retail credibility.
